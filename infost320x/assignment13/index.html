Training Tips

It is incredibly important when training for a race to ensure that you've paced yourself properly. Don't try and cram like it's an exam - remember the adage - "it's a marathon, not a sprint".

This is one of the most important things that new runners need to realize, and the Milwaukee Running Club aims to let everyone know.
